Idea Gallery

Data on its own is not as helpful as it could be. To be truly useful, data needs to be sorted, analyzed, and transformed in new applications, visualizations, and stories. The Idea Gallery on OpenDataPhilly features some of the most engaging and interesting uses of open data. If you have created a beautiful visualization or have used Philadelphia data in other inspiring ways and you think it's worth sharing with others in the Idea Gallery, let us know at info@opendataphilly.org.
